My Guide To Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is a multiplayer farming simulation game. After your grandpa passed away, you inherit a plot of land in a small town called Pelican Town and it is your job to restore the farm.

Farm Types

There are many types of farms, the one you choose will greatly impact your game play. I personally recommend Standard farm if you are a beginner.

Seasons

There are four seasons in a year, each lasting 28 days. One day is approx. 15 minutes in real life. Some crops makes more money than others and these are the most profitable crop for each seasons. You can also befriend villagers by giving them their favorite treats on their birthdays!

Spring

Most Profitable Crop: Strawberries

Summer

Most Profitable Crop: Blueberries

Fall

Most Profitable Crop: Cranberries

Winter

You can't plant in the fall!!! Time to up your other skilsets.
